PowderGuide

In the summer of 2023 me and croox.com started developing a weather service, dedicated to winter mountain activities, in particular skitouring and freeride, and it is operational ever since winter 2023/24 on powderguide.com. We process and visualize the ICON numerical weather forecasts run by the german weather service DWD. In its third version we are presenting these forecasts on a three-dimensional map.

Expedition Meteorology

In spring of 2026 I started to prepare myself for working as expedition meteorologist. My primary tool here are PowderGrams, which are my interpretation of a meteogram. I managed to operationalize their production and with those I am confident to deliver vital weather reports for any location in the world.
